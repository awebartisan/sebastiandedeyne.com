JavaScript Framework Diet

JavaScript frameworks are great, but overused. Adding small bits of interactivity to an interface shouldn't mean installing kilobytes of dependencies and introducting complex build tools.

It's time for a diet. I'm challenging you to build something without a framework. This series is meant to teach the foundations of building apps without a client-side framework.

Series under development, posts are added on a weekly-ish basis.
